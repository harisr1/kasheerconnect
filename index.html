Core Features

Everything you need
to connect.

Kasheer Connect is built around four pillars — discovery, connection, conversation, and safety.

🧭
Discover Nearby People
Browse profiles of real people from across Kashmir using GPS-based discovery with smart filters for interests, age, and location.
🤝
Connection Requests
Send and receive connection requests with full control. Accept who you want, decline who you don't — always on your terms.
💬
Real-Time Chat
Instant messaging with voice notes, reply threads, read receipts, and haptic feedback — smooth and fast.
🔒
Privacy & Safety
Block, report, and screenshot detection baked in. Your safety is not an afterthought — it's in the architecture.
How It Works

Three steps to connect.

Getting started on Kasheer Connect takes less than two minutes.

01
Create Your Profile
Sign up with your Kashmir location and build a profile that truly represents you — photos, stories, and bio.
02
Discover Users
Browse the Discover feed to find people nearby, filtered by interests, age, and distance across the valley.
03
Connect & Chat
Send a connection request. Once accepted, chat instantly with full messaging features.

Our Vision

Built for Kashmir.

Not a generic app with Kashmir in the name. Every feature, every decision — made for our people.

Local First

Only Kashmiri users. GPS-verified signup ensures every profile belongs to someone from the valley. Your community, no outsiders.

Safety by Design
